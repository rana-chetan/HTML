Geology Section

A geological section, also known as a geological cross-section, is a vertical slice or diagram that represents the arrangement of geological features (like rock layers, faults, and folds) below the Earth's surface. These sections are typically constructed from information gathered on geological maps and help visualize the three-dimensional structure of an area.
